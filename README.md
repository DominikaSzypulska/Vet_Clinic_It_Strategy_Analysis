# 🐾 Vet Clinic – IT Strategy & System Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a business analysis and IT strategy for the digital transformation of a veterinary clinic.

The goal was to identify operational challenges and propose modern IT solutions to improve efficiency, data management, and service quality.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Improve operational efficiency of the clinic
* Enhance data management and accessibility
* Optimize communication between departments
* Introduce modern IT solutions supporting medical and administrative processes

---

## 🧩 Business Analysis

The project includes a comprehensive analysis of the organization:

* Organizational structure and roles
* Current IT systems and processes
* Identification of inefficiencies and operational gaps
* Communication and data flow analysis

---

## 💡 Proposed IT Solutions

* Implementation of an integrated patient management system
* Online appointment scheduling system
* Inventory and resource management system
* Automation of communication (notifications, reminders)
* Introduction of telemedicine services

---

## 📊 Strategic Analysis

* SWOT analysis
* Identification of business risks and opportunities
* Long-term digital transformation strategy
* Definition of strategic goals and development directions
